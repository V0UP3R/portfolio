About

My career at Bravo Serviços Logísticos is the culmination of a passion that began when I was just 13 years old: programming. From an early age, I discovered my fascination for solving problems and creating solutions through code, and that passion has remained with me to this day, now at the age of 21.

I started at Bravo as a TI intern, eager to put into practice everything I had learned over the years. I quickly realized that I had found the ideal place to develop my skills and grow professionally. With determination and dedication, in a short period of time, I was promoted to the position of systems analyst.

During this time, I had the opportunity to get involved in projects that were crucial to the company. A significant milestone was the creation, from scratch, of a virtual assistant, a solution that streamlined processes and improved internal and external communication at Bravo. Developing in Python, TypeScript, and Node.js, I was able to deliver this project on my own, which reinforced my confidence and ability to lead complex initiatives.

In addition to my experiences at Bravo, my journey as a self-taught programmer since the age of 13 has contributed significantly to my professional growth. Throughout these years, I immersed myself in various languages and technologies, honing my skills and expanding my knowledge. This passion for programming and my constant pursuit of learning have been instrumental in making me a versatile professional capable of tackling the challenges of the ever-evolving world of technology.

My dedication to expanding my expertise is also reflected in the certifications I have obtained on the Alura platform. I have invested time and effort in obtaining certifications in a wide range of languages, including Python, Java, Golang, React, Oracle, MySQL, Angular, PHP, JavaScript, NextJS, and Machine Learning. These certifications not only validate my skills but also demonstrate my motivation to stay updated and ready to tackle the ever-changing technological demands.

In addition to my professional journey, I also value my academic background. I have completed up to the seventh semester in Computer Engineering, having completed the first five semesters at Uniube and subsequently transferred to FMU. This combination of practical experience and theoretical knowledge strengthens my foundation and my ability to approach projects comprehensively and effectively.

Regardless of the environment I find myself in, my passion for programming and my unwavering drive for innovation always propel me to bring value and make a positive impact. I am confident that wherever I go, I will continue to contribute with creative solutions and exceptional results.

I am excited about the opportunities that the future holds for my career. With my enduring passion for programming, solid skills, and intrinsic motivation, I am ready to take on challenges, create value, and leave my mark wherever I go.
